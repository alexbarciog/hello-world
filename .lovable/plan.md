

# Fix AI-Generated Message Quality

## Problem
Messages generated by the AI SDR have 6 issues:
1. Feel AI-generated, not human
2. Overly elevated/fancy English vocabulary
3. Uses em-dash "—" (classic AI tell)
4. No line breaks / paragraphs
5. Too long
6. No clear question or purpose

## Root Cause
The system prompt in `generate-step-message/index.ts` lacks strict formatting and style constraints. It says "3-5 sentences" but doesn't enforce short, simple language or structure rules.

## Changes

### File: `supabase/functions/generate-step-message/index.ts`

**A. Rewrite the system prompt** with these additions:

1. **Simple vocabulary rule**: "Use simple, everyday English. Write at a 6th-grade reading level. No fancy words — use 'help' not 'leverage', 'use' not 'utilize', 'start' not 'initiate'."

2. **Ban em-dashes and AI patterns**: "NEVER use em-dashes (—). NEVER use semicolons. Use short sentences and periods instead. Avoid words like 'delighted', 'thrilled', 'fascinating', 'intriguing', 'remarkable', 'noteworthy'."

3. **Enforce paragraph structure**: "Split the message into 2-3 short paragraphs (1-2 sentences each). Use a blank line between paragraphs. Never write a wall of text."

4. **Enforce brevity**: Change "3-5 sentences max" to "2-4 sentences max, under 50 words total". Add: "If you can say it in fewer words, do it."

5. **Clear question/CTA rule**: "Every message MUST end with ONE clear, simple question. The question should be easy to answer with a yes/no or a short reply. Examples: 'Worth a quick chat?' or 'Does this sound like something you're dealing with?'"

6. **Add few-shot examples** in the system prompt to anchor the style:

```
GOOD example:
"{{first_name}}, saw your post about scaling the sales team at {{company}}. We've been helping similar teams cut their ramp time in half.

Would it make sense to compare notes?"

BAD example:
"I was truly fascinated to come across your remarkable insights regarding the intricacies of scaling — it's clear that you possess a deep understanding of the challenges that organizations face when navigating growth trajectories."
```

**B. Post-process the output** (line ~147): After receiving the AI response, sanitize it:
- Replace all `—` with `, ` or `. `
- Trim if over 300 characters (truncate at last complete sentence)

This is a single-file change to `supabase/functions/generate-step-message/index.ts`.
